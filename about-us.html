About Our Charity

Where We Work

As a global movement, operating in over 120 countries, last year the Save the Children movement directly reached 56.3 million children.

In South Africa, we work across five provinces – Gauteng, Mpumalanga, Free State, Limpopo and KwaZulu- Natal, and our programmes have supported 66,826 children directly this year. In addition, we meaningfully engaged with children’s families and broader support systems and so, 83,437 people in total benefited from our programmes. Our staff are experts providing practical support in the field to children, creating impacts at scale.

With the generosity of thousands of South Africans, we work hard to protect children from harm and help them access quality education and health services.

With more than 65 years working with children, we are at the forefront of saving children’s lives and creating lasting change for children and their families through our long-term development programs. Watch our videos, view images and read stories from those who are benefiting from our programs in South Africa.
