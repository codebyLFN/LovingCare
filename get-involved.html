IF YOU ARE A COMPANY

Why work with LovingCare Inc?



LovingCare Inc is a dependable and respectable organization that can provide unrivaled expertise and on-the-ground support to businesses looking to take the next step in corporate responsibility and become a child rights advocate.

We have strong ties to grassroots communities and civil society, but we also have world-class experience working with UN agencies, donors, and governments. Every day, we communicate with children all over the world, giving us firsthand knowledge of their rights and needs. We are a global leader in integrating child rights into sustainability agendas and core business activities.

Through pioneering work on the rights and business of children and through, for example, our relationship to China 's child rights and business centre, we are now a strategic and well-gone player in understanding the meaning of rights of children in the daily business environment.

Drawing on these experiences, as well as the unrivaled local knowledge provided by LovingCare local offices worldwide, a global structure is now ready to provide comprehensive service to your company. Whether on a global, regional, or local scale, collaborating with LovingCare Inc allows you to develop sustainable solutions that are good for business, good for development, and good for children.
